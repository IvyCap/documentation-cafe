Bulletish Coffee

An emulsion of coffee, MCT oil, and coconut oil

Recipe

Ingredients:

Equipment

2 cups French Press Coffee

Mixing Container (We us a 6 cup metal measuring cup)

2 tbs Coconut Oil

Hand Mixer or Whisk

1 tbs MCT Oil

  1. Prepare a French Press coffee

  2. Add coconut oil and MCT oil in to a mixing container

  3. Pour the coffee into the mixing container

  4. With a hand mixer blend the mixture together

  5. Once the oils are thoroughly mixed with the coffee it is ready to be served
